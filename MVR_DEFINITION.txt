In high-context markets, Minimum Viable Relationships (MVR) is the non-negotiable prerequisite to MVP; without MVR, an MVP is an invalid experiment.

MVR evaluates whether a market actor has enough trust, permission, embeddedness, stakeholder readiness, and evidence support to proceed.

The MVR API is the machine-callable relational-readiness layer for AI agents and decision systems operating in emerging and high-context markets.
