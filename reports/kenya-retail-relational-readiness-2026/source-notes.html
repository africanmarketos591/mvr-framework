Source Notes and Release Boundaries

These notes describe the public source treatment and release boundaries for the 2026 Kenya Retail Relational Readiness Report.

Public Verdict Boundary

The public report is based on the MVR Core API v6.32.0 decision-check outputs. The verdict is pilot_only for Nairobi City County plus the Kiambu Ruaka-Ruiru-Thika corridor. It is not a national Kenya retail authorization, not a credit decision, not legal or regulatory advice, and not a standalone investment approval document.

The evidence pack is evidence-ready for human-reviewed bounded pilot diligence. It is not authorized as a sole basis for investment, credit approval without committee review, regulatory certification, or national scale.

Evidence Handling

Raw records are retained in the African Market OS internal audit archive. External verification requires controlled access to the redacted evidence bundle, fieldwork instruments, source screenshots or archived pages, anonymized source registers, and the methodology appendix.

All survey, telemetry, and social-listening evidence used in the public brief is presented only in aggregated or redacted form; no personally identifiable respondent or merchant data is disclosed.

Driver files, raw engine outputs, source registers, private JSON audit artifacts, calibration assets, and the protected MVR runtime source are not published.

Market-Sizing Note

The reconciled public benchmark for the food/grocery-related retail opportunity is treated as a bounded proxy, not as a complete national retail valuation. The prior speculative USD 56.4B inference is superseded. The safer USD 18.1B benchmark combines Euromonitor/USDA category baselines and should not be used as a full Kenya retail market size unless category overlap is separately reconciled.
